Dining Dollars Tracker

See exactly where your money is going: restaurants, groceries, or on-campus spots. Stay on pace all semester.

Swipe Manager

Our Mission

College dining plans are expensive and confusing. Students consistently overspend early in the semester or waste money at the end. NomNom was built to change that, giving every student the clarity to eat well, spend wisely, and enjoy campus food without the stress.
